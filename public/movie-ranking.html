The Best Movies According to Saravana

My Top 3 Movies of All time.

Vaaranam Aayiram

The movie describes about the life of Suriya and the love and affection he got from his Father.

Bombay

The movie tells about the inter-religion love life of Shekar and Shaira Banu and how they overcome their parents opposition and marrying eachother. The second half of the movie describes about the religion riot happening in Bombay where they settled.

Sillunu Oru Kadhal

This movies shows about the marriage life of Gautham and Kundhavi. It also tells the beautiful and blissful past relationship of Gautham with Aishu.
